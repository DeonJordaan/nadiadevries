Everyone wants to be healthy, but what does 'healthy' even mean?
Scroll ↓

For many people the usual suspects might come to mind— eating more fruit and vegetables, exercising regularly, quitting smoking or drinking and the usual: shedding a few extra kilos.

The world health organisation takes a broader view, describing it as "a state of complete physical, mental and social well-being"
