Becoming healthy

The world health organisation describes it as

"a state of complete physical, mental and social well-being and not merely the absence of disease or infirmity"

By choosing to live a life that honours your physical, mental and social well-being, you will promote the human organism's potential for healing itself, thus taking control over your own health and wellbeing.

By paying attention to your body, you can take note of unhealthy habits before they accumulate and cause long-term damage

This is where I can be your guide, by acting as an agent of long-term behaviour change.

You can nourish your health. But nourishment does not always mean nutrition.

Coaching

We will work with 3 core concepts: bio-individuality, primary food and secondary food.

We'll work through these core concepts together and, using self-inquiry and reflection, meet you where you are at in each of them. We can focus on elements that can impact markers of health such as stress, weight and energy levels, to name a few.

By making yourself accountable, I will guide you on your journey to a balanced way of life.

It will be your responsibility and willingness to commit that will be the key to unlocking sustainable health through long-term behaviour change.
